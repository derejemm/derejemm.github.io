# 👤 About Me

Hello! I'm **Dereje Mechal Molla**, a researcher and developer specializing in **Vehicular Communication (V2X)**, **Software-Defined Networking (SDN)**, and **Machine Learning**.

I currently focus on **hybrid V2X systems for multi-modal transportation**, where vehicles dynamically select wireless connectivity technologies for reliable and robust communication.

---

### 🎓 Education
- PhD in Computer Science — Université Gustave Eiffel, Champs-sur-Marne, France
- MSc in Computer and Communication Networks Engineering (Telematica) — Politecnico di Torino, Turin, Italy
- BSc in Electrical Engineering — Hawassa University, Hawassa Ethiopia

---

### 🔬 Research Interests
- V2X communication & ITS-G5/C-V2X integration  
- SDN-based vehicular decision-making  
- AI-driven latency and QoS prediction  
- 5G/6G and edge computing for intelligent mobility
